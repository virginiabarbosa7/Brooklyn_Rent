Brooklyn Community District 2 still has one of the fastest growing rent in the Brooklyn Borough

Many reasons, including the growing population in the region, has contributed for this increase

For the past years, Community District 2, in Brooklyn, has been known as one of the fastest-growing rent prices in the New York area. The chart bellow shows how in the past eight years prices increased significantly.

One of the main reasons for this increase has been that since the financial crises of 2008-09 the city's population has grown from half-a-million residents to 8.6 million, while the number of new housing units grew by only 100,000 - a fraction of what was actually needed. By consequence, rents has risen by over 24% on average over the same period.
